Visualization


Quantified
Self

Individuals can combine their information from periodic healthcare visits and personal health logs or continuous wearable device data to learn more about how these wellness features relate to each other.

  • Click Here to demo the quantified self implementation


Clinical Evaluation

Physicians use changes in particular sets of health features over time to develop prognoses, plan intervention regimens, and assess the response to past interventions. To address the challenge in efficient information retrieval that physicians currently face, we demonstrate an add-on that mines the underlying data repository to retrieve the most relevant data for a selected context.

  • Click Here to demo healthcare provider full implementation.
  • Click Here to demo healthcare provider ICD-9 CM implementation.
  • Click Here to demo healthcare provider real-time implementation.


Population Health Management

Clinical researchers simultaneously investigate cohorts of patients to discover treatment resposnes, and quality improvement designers simultaneously monitor units of patients to find outliers that require special attention. For these cases we demonstrate an app for visualizing batches of patients in a selected cohort.
